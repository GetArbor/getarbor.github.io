Our transparent approach

Arbor allows you to open an account with a reputable bank in your company's name without changing the way you do business.

Arbor took a team with more than 50 years of experience in financial compliance and partnered with regulators to develop an industry specific 300-point auditing and monitoring framework.

Arbor works with your company to become compliant with our program. Once compliant, Arbor opens a bank account with one of our banking partners in your company’s name.

The bank account stays open permanently. As long as your company demonstrates good faith to remain compliant with Arbor’s ongoing auditing and monitoring program, the bank account will not be shut down.

 

How It Works

Arbor's three part process works well for both small and large dispensaries

1

Prepare for Inspection

Arbor works with your company to make sure your business practices are in line with our compliance program.

Arbor helps prepare documentation that will help regulators understand your business.

2

Onsite Audit

Arbor spends between three and five days at your company inspecting your processes, reviewing your business practices and talking to your staff.

After your audit is complete, you receive an audit score and a bank account in your company’s name.

3

Ongoing Monitoring

Arbor reviews daily videotape of your operations, conducts spot compliance checks, secret shoppers, and reviews your general ledger.

You receive monthly reports verifying your compliance and are immediately alerted to any discrepancies.
